We are a government funded organization with a goal of reducing plastic pollution, our vision is to have a net-zero carbon world.



What is plastic pollution?

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest problems in the world. Plastic Pollution is the pollution of waste plastics used by people, industries, and for commercial purposes. There are over 5 trillion plastic particles on the surface of our oceans! This is only the plastic in our oceans, but we have over 27 million tons of plastic in landfills around the world! This is terrible as plastic takes hundreds of years to disintergrate, and it harms the ecosystem, waterways while it is in landfills. This happens because the toxins from the plastics flow into waterways when rain happens, killing ecosystems.

Process

This is our Solution

There are

Drones

Drones flying in a specific community, and they map out all of the pieces of plastic in the area. Drones use computer vision algorithm to map out all of the the pieces of plastic in an enviroment. It also uses other computer vision algorithmw to ensure that they do not collide with other objects.

Robots

The data collected by the drones is used by the robots to go to those locations and pick up the garbage. To do this the robots use a special wide vacume allowing it to suck all the plastic. If the robots are out of energy, they can go to the closest charging station.

Smart Recycle Bins

When the robot reaches maximum capacity, it goes to smart garbage bins which shred the plastic and use it to generate renewable energy. There is also an app which serves 2 purposes. You can "order" a robot to your location where you can feed the plastic. The app also shows the locations of the bins for people in the nearby area, so they can dispose plastic there.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do our robots naviagate around streets?

    EarthAI's robots are being able to navigate through the real world using computer vision algorithms using Convolution Neural Network to avoid different objects in a specific enviroment. It also runs on an autonomous networking using AI to make sure that a robot will not collide with another robot in an area. The reason why we want to use an autonomous network is because we don't want people in datacenters mangaging large networks.

  • What will happen if the robot discharges?

    If the EarthAI's robots is low on battery it will be able to go to the nearest charging station when its battery goes under 20%. People at those charging stations can charge the battery of the robot. This proccess is similar to how to fill up gas in our cars today.

  • How will EarthAI's robot be able dispose the plastic into the recycle bin?

    Since the recycle bins are created specially for the robot there will be a pipe at the bottom of the recycle bin, where the robot can attact itself to. Once it gets attached to the recycle bin, then it will tranfer all of the plastic threw the pipe into the recycle bin.
